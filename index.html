Approach

External, lightweight and non invasive

The deterministic timing layer sits outside your existing stack and wraps around processes and execution paths. It observes and stabilises how events move through the system, without modifying payloads, business logic or authoritative timestamps.

DTL is delivered as a low weight API or sidecar service. Integration is designed to be reversible and straightforward to trial, with clear rollback behaviour and minimal operational risk.

Behaviour

Drift, jitter and tail behaviour

DTL focuses on timing behaviour between events. It targets drift, jitter and long tail latency, aiming to keep flows inside a tighter and more predictable window under load.

Benchmarks show reductions in variance and tail heaviness while keeping medians unchanged. Full result packs and test notes are available under NDA where needed.
