FoundScript DTL

Deterministic timing for real time, event driven systems under load.

External timing layer that reduces variance amplification between call and execution, improving flow predictability under bursty conditions without touching payloads or business logic. Delivered as a lightweight API or sidecar service.

Approach

External, lightweight and non invasive

The deterministic timing layer sits outside your existing stack and wraps around processes. It observes and stabilises how events move through the system without modifying payloads, business logic or timestamps.

DTL is delivered as a lightweight API or sidecar service. No changes to core infrastructure are required; integration is designed to be reversible and straightforward to trial.

Behaviour

Drift, jitter and tail behaviour

DTL focuses on timing behaviour between events. It targets drift, jitter and long tail latency, aiming to keep flows inside a tighter and more predictable window under bursty load.

Evaluation packs cover variance and tail behaviour in representative workloads. Details are shared under NDA where needed.
