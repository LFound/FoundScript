Approach

External, lightweight and non invasive

The deterministic timing layer sits outside your existing stack and wraps around processes. It observes and stabilises how events move through the system without modifying payloads, business logic or timestamps.

DTL is delivered as a lightweight API or sidecar service. Integration is reversible and designed to be straightforward to trial.

Behaviour

Drift, jitter and tail behaviour

DTL focuses on timing behaviour between events. It targets drift, jitter and long tail latency, keeping flows inside a tighter and more predictable window under bursty load.

Evaluation packs cover variance and tail behaviour in representative workloads. Details shared under NDA where required.
