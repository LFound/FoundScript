Approach

External, lightweight and non invasive

The deterministic timing layer sits outside your existing stack and wraps around processes. It observes and stabilises how events move through the system, without modifying payloads, business logic or exchange timestamps.

DTL is delivered as a low weight API or sidecar service. No changes to core infrastructure are required; integration is designed to be reversible and straightforward to trial.

Behaviour

Drift, jitter and tail behaviour

DTL focuses on timing behaviour between events. It targets drift, jitter and long tail latency, aiming to keep flows inside a tighter and more predictable window under load.

Early benchmark tests show reductions in variance and tail heavy behaviour while keeping median timings broadly unchanged. Detailed result packs are available under discussion and NDA.
