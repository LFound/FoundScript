Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

FoundScript Ltd, registered in England and Wales.

FoundScript Ltd (“FoundScript”, “we”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect only the minimum personal information necessary and process it only for legitimate business purposes related to enquiries, support, evaluation and service delivery.

1. Data controller

FoundScript Ltd is the data controller responsible for the processing of personal data described in this policy.

2. Information we collect

We do not use analytics tools, advertising trackers or profiling tools on this website. We do not intentionally collect personal information automatically when you visit our website.

The personal information we process is limited to information you voluntarily provide, including when you:

  • email us directly
  • request support or assistance
  • enquire about an evaluation, pilot, deployment or integration

3. How we use your information

We use personal information you provide only to:

  • respond to enquiries
  • provide technical or operational support
  • manage evaluations, pilots or deployment discussions
  • fulfil contractual, legal or regulatory obligations where applicable

We do not sell or rent personal information. We do not share personal information for advertising purposes.

4. Legal basis for processing

Where UK GDPR or GDPR applies, we process personal data only where:

  • you have given consent
  • processing is necessary to respond to your request
  • processing is necessary for legitimate business purposes, including support, evaluation and service delivery
  • processing is necessary to comply with legal obligations

5. Data storage and security

Correspondence and related business records may be stored within our company email and business systems. We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, loss, misuse or disclosure.

6. Data retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, including support, evaluation and business record-keeping, or as required to comply with legal obligations.

7. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request to:

  • access the personal data we hold about you
  • request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • request deletion of personal data
  • withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, contact info@foundscript.com.

8. Children’s privacy

Our services are not intended for children, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 16.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page together with the revised date.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, contact:
