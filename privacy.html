Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

FoundScript Ltd (“FoundScript”, “we”) is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal personal information and process it only for purposes you have requested.

1. Information we collect

We do not use cookies, analytics tools, tracking scripts or third-party data collectors. We do not automatically collect personal information when you visit our website.

The only personal information we process is information you voluntarily provide, including when you:

  • email us directly
  • request support or assistance
  • enquire about a DTL pilot, evaluation or integration

2. How we use your information

We use voluntarily provided information solely to:

  • respond to enquiries
  • provide operational or technical support
  • manage evaluation or pilot discussions
  • fulfil contractual or regulatory obligations (if applicable)

We do not sell, rent or share your information with third parties.

3. Legal basis for processing (UK GDPR / GDPR)

We process your data only where:

  • you have given explicit consent; or
  • it is necessary to respond to your request; or
  • it is required for legitimate business purposes such as support or pilot configuration.

4. Data storage

Email correspondence may be securely stored within our company email systems. Reasonable technical and organisational measures are used to protect this data.

5. Your rights

You may request at any time to:

  • access the personal data we hold about you
  • request correction or deletion
  • withdraw consent to processing

To exercise these rights, contact: info@foundscript.com

6. Children’s privacy

Our services are not intended for children and we do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 16.

7. Changes to this policy

Updates to this policy will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
