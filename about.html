About FoundScript

Infrastructure for reliable AI systems.

FoundScript builds tools for AI and software systems that need to reason, remain stable and be observable in production.

The company is focused on a simple problem: as systems become more automated, distributed and agentic, engineers need better ways to understand state, control behaviour and recover from failure.

Where reliability breaks down

FoundScript focuses on failure points that appear when AI, software and operational workflows become too complex to manage through dashboards or prompt changes alone.

How FoundScript approaches the problem

Modern AI systems are becoming more capable, but also more difficult to inspect and control. Long running workflows can lose context, agents can act on incomplete information and failures can propagate before engineering teams understand what changed.

Rather than focusing solely on outputs, FoundScript develops infrastructure that makes system behaviour easier to understand while workflows are running through explicit reasoning state, runtime control and workflow observability.

Reason Track assumptions, transitions and reasoning state across complex workflows.
Control Apply deterministic control before instability propagates through connected systems.
Observe Surface ambiguity, missing context and workflow drift before execution.

Frequently asked questions

What does a technical evaluation look like?

Every engagement starts with understanding your environment and the problem you are trying to solve. Depending on the product, evaluations may use a representative sandbox, a customer-provided test environment or a staged deployment against a non-production workload.

How are FoundScript products implemented?

Products are designed to be lightweight and non-invasive. Depending on the product, deployment may be through APIs, sidecars, standalone services or deeper integration where appropriate. Existing systems remain authoritative throughout deployment.

Do we need to replace our existing systems?

No. FoundScript products are designed to complement existing infrastructure rather than replace it. Initial evaluations are intended to minimise operational risk and can typically be rolled back without affecting existing systems.

Where are FoundScript products applicable?

FoundScript products are designed for AI workflows, distributed systems, operational software, optimisation pipelines and other environments where reasoning, runtime behaviour or workflow observability become operational challenges.

How long does a typical evaluation take?

This depends on the product and environment, but most evaluations begin with a short technical discussion followed by a representative proof of concept or controlled evaluation before any production deployment.

How is commercial engagement structured?

Initial technical discussions are exploratory. Commercial engagement depends on the product, evaluation scope and deployment requirements and is agreed before any formal project begins.

Are benchmark results available?

Controlled evaluation summaries are published where available. Independent production validation continues as products mature and additional evidence becomes available.
